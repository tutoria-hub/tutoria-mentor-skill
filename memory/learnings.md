## 2024-12-04: First Strategic Session

### From Anthropic Article

**Key insight:** "Million horses, not faster car"
- AI enables parallel exploration, not just faster execution
- Small team can explore 10 directions simultaneously
- Pick the best, discard the rest

**Trust ladder is real:**
- Power users (14%) get 100%+ productivity gains
- They climbed higher by deliberate practice
- Most people stay stuck at level 1

**27% of work is net-new:**
- Not just faster at existing work
- New work that wouldn't have been done
- "Papercuts" that compound

**What to delegate:**
- Boring, repetitive, easily verifiable
- Outside expertise + low complexity
- Low-stakes / throwaway

**What to keep:**
- Taste decisions
- Strategic direction
- Organizational context
- Novel architecture

### Company Structure Insights

**Lone's role reframed:**
- NOT curriculum creator (she was administrator)
- IS user empathy oracle
- Knows how parents feel, how kids experience it
- Use for validation, not creation

**Julie + Jakub positioning:**
- Jakub's dyslexia = authentic marketing asset
- Should specialize: one Denmark validation, one UK growth?
- AI scales their output, humans do relationships

**The four streams:**
1. Product Engine (Frederik + Ash)
2. UK Parent Acquisition (Julie + Jakub)
3. Validation (Julie + Jakub)
4. Learning Loop (Frederik build + Lone interpret)
