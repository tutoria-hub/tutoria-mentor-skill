# Growth Playbook

How to get UK parents. First 10 customers and beyond.

**Last updated:** 2024-12-04

---

## Target Customer

**Primary:** UK parents of dyslexic children (ages 5-12)

**Profile:**
- Child diagnosed or suspected dyslexic
- Frustrated with school support (waiting lists, limited help)
- Willing to invest time and money in intervention
- Likely already researching online

**Why UK:**
- Curriculum is British English
- Large English-speaking market
- Active online parent communities
- Can expand to US/international later

## First 10 Customers Plan

**Phase 1: Manual & Free (Customers 1-5)**

1. Join "Parent Dyslexia Support Group" on Facebook
2. Be genuinely helpful (answer questions, share knowledge)
3. When someone posts "help, my child is struggling with phonics":
   - DM them
   - Offer free access in exchange for feedback
4. Do this 10 times, get 5 to actually use it
5. Collect testimonials

**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks

**Phase 2: Warm Outreach (Customers 6-10)**

1. Post in groups (with permission) about what you built
2. Jakub's story: "I'm dyslexic, I built this to help kids like me"
3. Offer early-access pricing
4. More testimonials

**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks

**Phase 3: Paid Acquisition (Customer 11+)**

Now you have:
- Real testimonials
- Proven messaging (what made people sign up)
- Landing page with social proof

Ads will convert better.

## Content Strategy

**Goal:** Be found when parents search for dyslexia help.

**Content types:**
1. Educational (how phonics works, what Orton-Gillingham is)
2. Empathy (stories from dyslexic adults, parent experiences)
3. Practical (tips for helping at home)
4. Product (how Tutoria works, case studies)

**AI-scaled workflow:**
- AI generates content ideas from trending topics
- AI drafts posts/articles
- Human (Jakub) reviews for authenticity
- Human adds personal story where relevant
- Schedule and publish

**Frequency:** 2-3 pieces per week
